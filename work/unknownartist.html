Unknown Artist Agency

Client // Wang Xin
Role // text scrapping
Tool // Python
Unknown Artist Agency is a project initiated by new media artist Wangxin. The goal is to discover unknown artists and help them to gain exposure through her exhibition.
I did text scrapping on major archive websites such as artwanted, artfinder, faso to get data of over 10,000 unknown artists. The artist info got printed out in paper, folded into pink balls and scattered on the floor to form a pool of unknown artist.
The project was exhibited in de Sarthe Gallery, Hongkong from 10/21 to 11/18, 2017.
