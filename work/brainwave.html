X in White

Client // indivisual work
Role // concept development
Tool // Neurosky, Raspberry Pi

X in White is a psychedelic light experiment connected with your brain activity .Light frequency and color change human's brain status dramatically. In the emmersive light space, users handed over their vulnerbility to be hypotized and play with their self-control consciously and unconsciously .

It was a collaborative project from X collective for Make+ Program in Xinchejian Hackerspace. It was exhibitied in Visual Center, M50 and French Consulate, Shanghai.
