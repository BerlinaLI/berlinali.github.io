BMW Data Art H5 Campaign

Client // BMW / Wieden+Kennedy
Role // User Experience
Tool // Axure
BMW Energy Art Campaign is a 3D generative data art project for Shanghai Marathon 2017. It collaborated with Joshua Davis, pioneer in generative art,as artistic director to transform user’s running data into an art piece on their device.
The campaign retrieved marathon runner’s data from selected running APPs and required them to enter their personal info for CRM. By working closely with back-end engineer and UI designer, we simplified the data input process to allow them focus on 3D generative art piece and share it on social media.
