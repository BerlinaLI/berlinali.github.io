Unknown Artist Agency

Client // Wang Xin
Role // text scrapping
Tool // Python
Unknown Artist Agency is a project initiated by new media artist Wangxin. The goal is to discover over 10,000 unknown artists and help them to get exposure through her exhibition. We printed out their info , folded papers into pink balls and invited visitors to discover them.
I did text scrapping on major artist archive websites such as artwanted, artfinder, faso.
The exhibition opens in de Sarthe Gallery, Hongkong from 10/21 to 11/18, 2017.
