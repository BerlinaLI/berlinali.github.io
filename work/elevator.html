Elevator Moment

Client // shaw
Role // browser prototype
Tool // Android Studio, React Native
For architect/artist shaw, he is obsessed with wechat moment to document objects as part of his art practice. He even saves all his moment as screen captures and put them together as a 30-meter long image. He requested us to make it interactive inside an elevator.
In collaboration with Luciano Hanyon, we maped data retrieved from embeded gyroscope sensor with the floor height and elevator door status. The image scrolled automatically corresponding to the direction and speed of the elevator, and even stoped when passengers entered or exited. It creates a poetic experience of viewing social media for elevator passenagers.
