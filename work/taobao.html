E-commerce Hacker Plug-in

Client // Pei Enen
Role // Front-end Development
Tool // jQuery, Chrome Plug-in
For most e-commerce websites, their recommendation algorithm are largely based on user's browsing history. By changing the history, we can change the way it targets us.
This plug-in places a bot to search products automatically accordng to a list of keywords users provide. The robot can enter keywords, select products, scroll product details, even click on other recommened products and then go for another loop. I teamed up with Luciano Hanyon to proviide technical support for artist Pie Enen.
