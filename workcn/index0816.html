某宝反广告黑客插件

Anti-Ad Plug-in

#e-commerce   #hack   #bot
该插件受艺术家佩恩恩委托开发。用户在插件界面输入关键字后，插件将模仿人类浏览页面的行为进行搜索，比如输入关键字，点击按钮，滚动页面，从而制造虚假的浏览记录，干扰某宝的广告和产品推荐机制。用户可以选择输入跟自己偏好相关或不相関的关键字来让电商网站更精准定位自己的需求或者达到相反的效果。插件只提供chrome浏览器版本。
The recommendation algorithm is largely based on user's browsing history. This plug-in places a bot to search products automatically accordng to a list of keywords users provide to hack the algorithm. By entering the keyword, scrolling product details and even clicking on other recommened product, the robot creates a browsing record to change the way e-commerce website targets you. I teamed up with Luciano Hanyon to proviide technical support for artist Pie Enen.

电梯朋友圈

Elevator Moment

#socialmedia   #interaction   #space
该作品受艺术家shaw委托开发。出于建筑师背景，shaw选择用米来衡量朋友圈的长度。通过手机传感器数据与楼层高度和运动趋势的匹配，让长达30米的朋友圈图像跟随楼层高度自动滚动，成为一个时刻在于环境交互的社交媒体。
Artist/architect s.h.a.w. tends to use meter to measure his social media. The installation displays his 30-meter wechat moment in a mobile phone which can be scrolled corresponding to the movement of the elevator. It creates a brand new experience to browse social media in public space. We developed an native APP to map phone sensor data with elevator movement.

Mao in the Sky

#RicherThanJeffKoons  #GenerativeArt
|
It is a web animation from my first art serie #RicherThanJeffKoons. The 100 yuan is the highest face value in Chinese currency. The pink and baby blue color set a cheerful and harmonic tone for visual. While there is a strong tension in the serious Mao profile, its parade-like generative movement and the sky which implicates nature and freedom. This serie is my reflection of the value of money during my career transition from a corporate employee to a self-employ digital artist.

Sound Flapping Bird

X in White

#brainwave #meditation #visualization
| |
这个装置追踪用户脑波活动输出现场的灯光变化。观众可以带上耳机体验语音催眠。正如在RBG中0到255之间，观众与装置产生微妙的关系，或沉浸式冥想或玩起挑战意志游戏。该作品曾在Basement6，视觉艺术中心（M50）和上海法国领事馆展览过。
This installation visualizes brainwave data of concentration and relaxation in lighting. It is an immersive space of meditation, a game of self-control and a data visualization project. It was a collabortion project from X collective for Make+ Program in Xinchejian. It was exhibitied in Visual Center, M50 and French Consulate, Shanghai.
