Berlina Li
/  Front-end Artist  /
Hi, I am a Shanghai-based front-end artist.
As a creative coder, I play a lot web interaction and animation especially on HMTL5.
Apart of being a tech catalyst to help artists to realize their project, I also create artwork myself. My major focus is creative application of technology and its implication to our everyday life. The hacker and the open source DIY philosophy have a big influnce on me after spending 4 years working in the Arduino community.
Send an email to berlina.lili(at)gmail(dot)com and say hi if you are interested in my work.
