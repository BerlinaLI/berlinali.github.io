Elevator Moment

Client // shaw
Role // browser prototype
Tool // Javascript, jQuery
For architect/artist shaw, he uses wechat moment to document his life as part of his art practice. He would like to have it displayed in an elevators. He even stitched all the screen captures together. The complete image measures 30 meters.
In this project, I collaborated with Luciano Hanyon. We retrieved data from embeded mobile sensors to allow the image scrolled automatically corresponding to the movement of the elevator. As a result, it creates a poetic experience for elevator passenagers to browser social media.
